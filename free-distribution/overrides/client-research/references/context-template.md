## Guidelines

1. **Preserve existing metadata**: If research.md already exists, keep the slug, created date, and owner from the original file.

2. **Be specific, not generic**: Avoid buzzwords like "innovative" or "cutting-edge". Use concrete descriptions of what the company does.

3. **Quantify when possible**: Include specific metrics, percentages, or dollar amounts in problems and case studies.

4. **Prioritize by ICP fit**: Select the top 3 assets and case studies most relevant to the primary target audience.

5. **Keep it scannable**: This section is for quick reference. Full details belong in the comprehensive research below it in the same file.
