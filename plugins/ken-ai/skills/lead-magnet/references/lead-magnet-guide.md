## Detailed Type Descriptions

### Software/Tools (Click-Based)
- **Calculators & Analyzers**: Input data, get personalized output. ROI calculators, benchmark tools, health checks.
- **Chrome Extensions**: Integrate into existing browser workflow. Best for tools that augment daily tasks.
- **Lovable Apps**: Single-purpose, beautifully designed tools that solve one workflow perfectly. Examples: ROI Calculator app, Team Health Monitor, Compliance Checker.
- **AI Tools**: Leverage AI to provide instant value. Examples: AI Sales Email Writer, Smart Contract Analyzer, Predictive Churn Model.

### Information (Click-Based)
- **Reports & Guides**: Industry data, trend analysis, best practices. Lower conversion but good for thought leadership.
- **Video Courses**: 5-day email courses or video series with actionable homework. Examples: "Scale Your SaaS to $10M ARR", "Master Product-Led Growth".
- **Mini-Courses**: Educational VSL or short video demo showing how the product/methodology works.

### Services (Reply-Based)
- **Audits & Consultations**: Personalized assessment of prospect's current state. Low scalability but high conversion.
- **Done-For-You Samples**: Process one file, analyze one account, run one campaign for free. Proves capability.
- **Implementation Assistance**: Set up one feature/integration for free. Creates switching cost and familiarity.

### Hybrid (Both)
- **Separated SaaS Features**: Extract your best feature as standalone. Full functionality, no strings attached. Examples: Data Visualization Tool from analytics platform, Workflow Builder from automation suite.
- **Templates & Frameworks**: Industry-specific, battle-tested templates. Examples: Sales Playbook Template, OKR Planning Framework, Customer Success Blueprint.

## Advanced Strategy Playbook

### Trojan Horse Method
**Goal**: Create tools that work standalone but work 10x better with the client's product.
**How**: Build a free tool that solves a real problem. Design it so the natural next step is using the full product.
**Examples**:
- A project template that's good alone but amazing with their project management tool
- A data visualization widget that works standalone but connects to their analytics platform
- A reporting template that manually works but auto-populates with their tool
**Best for**: SaaS products with strong core features

### Habit Builder
**Goal**: Design daily-use tools that create habits leading to the product.
**How**: Create something prospects use daily/weekly. Over time, they hit limitations that only the full product solves.
**Examples**:
- A daily standup bot that eventually needs the full collaboration platform
- A weekly email digest tool that becomes more powerful with the full analytics suite
- A simple task tracker that grows into needing project management
**Best for**: Products with daily/weekly use patterns

### Marketplace Model
**Goal**: Create a platform connecting prospects with solutions/services.
**How**: Build a platform that provides value to multiple parties. Prospects get free value from other vendors. You become the trusted curator and eventually the solution.
**Examples**:
- Marketplace with all businesses in a certain location (like Yelp)
- Vendor comparison tool for a specific industry
- Community platform connecting buyers and sellers
**Best for**: Platform businesses, marketplaces

### Developer Ecosystem Play
**Goal**: Build lead magnets that developers want to build on top of.
**How**: Create open-source tools with API access. Let developers extend functionality. Their additions bring their networks.
**Examples**:
- Open Source Analytics Library with your platform as the easiest hosting option
- API Testing Toolkit that works best with your infrastructure
- Integration Framework making it easy to connect tools through your platform
**Best for**: Developer tools, API-first products

### Predictive Power Play
**Goal**: Use AI/ML to predict future problems before they happen.
**How**: Analyze current state data. Predict future issues/opportunities. Position product as the solution.
**Examples**:
- Churn Prediction Model: Free analysis of customer base
- Revenue Forecasting Tool: Based on current pipeline
- Technical Debt Calculator: Predicts future development slowdowns
**Best for**: Analytics, monitoring, and prediction products

### AI Employee Preview
**Goal**: Offer an AI agent that does real work for them.
**How**: AI agent performs a specific role. Shows the future of work. Upgrade for more agents/capabilities.
**Examples**:
- AI SDR: Books meetings automatically
- AI Analyst: Generates reports from data
- AI Customer Success Manager: Monitors and engages customers
**Best for**: AI-powered products, automation platforms

## Reply-Based Offer Templates

### Done-For-You Sample Template
**Structure**: "We'll [specific deliverable] for [your company] - completely free. If you like the results, we can discuss [full engagement]."
**Key elements**:
- Specific, tangible deliverable (not vague "consultation")
- Zero commitment language
- Natural upsell path
**Example (Ken AI)**: "We'll send personalized emails to 5,000 of your ideal clients and book meetings for you. If you don't get 20+ meetings in 90 days, you don't pay."

### Guarantee Structure Template
**Structure**: "[Specific result] in [timeframe] or [consequence for you, not them]."
**Key elements**:
- Measurable outcome (number of meetings, % improvement, revenue generated)
- Clear timeframe
- Risk sits entirely with the seller
**Example**: "We guarantee 15 qualified demos in 60 days or we extend the campaign for free until you hit 15."

### Free Trial Framework Template
**Structure**: "Get [full/limited] access to [product] for [period]. No credit card. No commitment. [What happens after]."
**Key elements**:
- Clear scope (what they get)
- Clear timeline
- Clear exit (what happens when trial ends)
**Example**: "Full access to our analytics dashboard for 14 days. Connect your data in 5 minutes. Keep the reports even if you don't subscribe."

### Risk Reversal Pattern Template
**Structure**: "If [negative outcome], we [absorb the cost]. You only pay when [positive outcome]."
**Key elements**:
- Acknowledge the fear directly
- Absorb the specific risk they worry about
- Tie payment to results they want
**Example**: "If the leads don't convert, we replace them at no cost. You only pay per qualified meeting that actually shows up."

## Hook Formulas with Examples

### 1. The Shocking Stat
- "78% of B2B companies lose $50K+ annually to unqualified leads in their pipeline"
- "Only 23% of cold emails get opened - the other 77% are wasted budget"
- "The average sales team wastes 6 hours/week on manual data entry"

### 2. The Comparison
- "Companies using AI personalization get 3x more replies than those using mail merge"
- "Teams with automated onboarding retain 2.5x more customers in the first 90 days"
- "Businesses with a lead scoring model close 30% faster than those without"

### 3. The Hidden Cost
- "The average VP of Sales doesn't realize their team spends 40% of time NOT selling"
- "Most SaaS companies don't know they're losing $12K/month to preventable churn"
- "Your sales team is probably ignoring 60% of inbound leads because they can't prioritize"

### 4. The Competitive Intelligence
- "Your competitors are already using AI to personalize every cold email - here's how to catch up in 5 minutes"
- "Top-performing sales teams switched to async demos 6 months ago - here's what they learned"
- "The fastest-growing companies in your space all use this exact outbound playbook"

### 5. The Compliance Urgency
- "New email deliverability rules mean you need SPF/DKIM/DMARC by Q2 or face 90% spam rates"
- "GDPR enforcement is ramping up - here's a 5-minute check to see if your outreach is compliant"
- "Google's new sender requirements will block your emails unless you update by February"

## Lead Magnet Funnel (Click-Based)

```
Cold Email -> Landing Page -> Lead Magnet Delivery -> Email Nurture Sequence -> Sales Conversation
```

### Landing Page Best Practices
- Short and to the point
- Show the lead magnet in action (video/screenshots)
- Tools: ClickFunnels, GoHighLevel, Unbounce

### Email Nurture Sequence (3-7 emails after delivery)
1. Delivery + Quick Win
2. Success Story
3. Common Mistake
4. Advanced Strategy
5. FAQ/Objections
6. Direct Offer

## Critical Reminders

- The best lead magnets feel like products, not marketing
- If it would take more than 2 weeks to build, propose an MVP version
- Always consider: "Would they pay $50-500 for this if they didn't know us?"
- The lead magnet should make the core product feel like the obvious next step
- Reply-based offers need zero friction - no landing pages, no forms, just reply
- Guarantee-based offers work best when the guarantee is specific and measurable
- Stack multiple value elements when possible (guarantee + sample + timeline)
